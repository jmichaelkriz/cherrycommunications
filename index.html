About

Elizabeth (Beth) Cherry

Founder, Cherry Communiactions

Elizabeth (Beth) Cherry is a seasoned marketing and communications professional, with over 20+ years of agency and in-house experience. Serving as counsel to C-Suite leaders for much of her career, her public relations experience runs the gamut—from strategic comms to day-to-day operations, and everything in between. Her work includes authority building, executive comms, media relations, and partnerships, and previous roles range from inaugural comms leadership, to helping to run a digital marketing and influencer agency, to being an "earned media whisperer" for other agencies, to running her own PR firm.

Ms. Cherry has secured press briefings and media wins for clients with outlets such as The Washington Post, Forbes, POLITICO, ProPublica, The Wall Street Journal, CNBC, USA Today, and numerous trade publications.

Most recently, Ms. Cherry led the branding and identity efforts for reDesign, an award-winning national education consultancy, and provided comms leadership to an international trade association in the medical technology field. Prior to that, she held a leadership role with Well Spirit Collective, an influencer and marketing agency. She also worked for several start-up companies in the edtech space and got her start at Smithsonian Magazine.

She graduated from the University of Maryland in College Park with undergraduate degrees in History and Anthropology, and a minor in Italian studies. She lives in the Washington, D.C. area, pursues health and wellness with a passion, and loves to travel and explore the great outdoors.
